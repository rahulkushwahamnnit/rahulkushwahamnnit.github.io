All outside participants of AVISHKAR 2014 will be provided with accommodation .
We will provide you with bedding, bed sheet, pillow, blanket, mosquito repellant etc.
You are expected to follow the discipline of NIT Allahabad during your stay here.
All the participants opting for accomodation will be provided with bedding from 4nd night to 8th
afternoon 12 o'clock. If you want to stay longer, you will have to give prior information to the
hospitality team.Accommodation will be on the first come first serve basis. So it is advisable to
share your travel plans with us well in advance.

How do I reach Allahabad?

Allahabad, the headquarters of North Central Railways, is well connected by rail to all parts of India. To find trains to Allahabad Junction, visit http://www.indianrail.gov.in. Allahabad is also connected via air. Other nearby airports are in Varanasi and Lucknow from where you can reach Allahabad by hourly bus services or by train.
