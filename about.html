About We collaborate with forward-thinking brands to achieve business objectives through strategy, creativity and technology.

Union is a digital–first agency. We create experiences that help businesses thrive.

We believe that in order to reach and engage with today’s highly connected consumers, all successful marketing programs should lead with digital. We partner with many of the Southeast region’s top brands to craft impactful digital marketing platforms, content and campaigns. We are confident in working with clients ranging from Fortune 500 companies to local startups. The experiences we create dutifully align with our client’s objectives and goals — and deliver measurable results.

Back in February, I wrote a love letter post about An Event Apart. Karen McGrane's presentation "The Mobile Content Mandate" made it quite clear that the mobile revolution is already here. Instead of needing a home computer and an internet provider, all that's needed is a phone with a data plan. With an increasing number of web-aware devices comes the need to test on a wide variety of them. Not only are there more devices out there, but the differences between their screen dimensions are vast.
