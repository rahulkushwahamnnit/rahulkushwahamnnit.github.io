Union (formerly Studiobanks) Expands Team with Two New Designers

In Company News by Union

Union (formerly Studiobanks) has added two talented designers to their team. Matt Daniels and Andy Mindler will focus on web design, illustration and user interface design for interactive websites and mobile applications.

Daniels earned a B.A. in Fine Arts at UNC Charlotte and has more than six years of design experience, including the illustration of two children's books.

"We are fortunate and proud of the growth our agency has experienced," said Banks Wilson, Union' president and creative director. "Matt and Andy bring great talent and energy to our team, and are already producing impressive work for our clients."

Union (formerly Studiobanks) Leads Interactive at Charlotte ADDY Awards

In Company News by Union

This year's Charlotte ADDY Awards Show & Preview Gallery brought together a record 275 industry professionals to celebrate creative excellence in our community. The Mint Museum Uptown provided a gorgeous setting that matched the evening's award-winning, artistic achievements.

During the Awards Show, Union (formerly Studiobanks) was presented with the following awards:

  • Gold ADDY: Parker Poe Holiday Microsite
  • Gold ADDY: Union Holiday Microsite
  • Silver ADDY: CMA Awards Website
  • Silver ADDY: CMA Country Christmas Microsite
  • Silver ADDY: Muzak A/V Systems Website
  • Silver ADDY: Reel-Scout Marketing Website
  • Silver ADDY: Reel-Scout Search Website
  • Silver ADDY: SAFER Barrier Website
  • Silver ADDY: Varrow Website

Awards are an important part of our industry as they benchmark creative excellence and online success. We happily accepted these awards on behalf of the collabortive efforts and partnerships with our awesome clients.
