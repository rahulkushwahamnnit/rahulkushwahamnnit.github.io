Two New Additions

In Company News by Union

Union (formerly Studiobanks) is proud to announce the completion of two new projects. The Bamboo Room, a singular, blues club located in Lake Worth, FL, serves up authentic blues music five days a week. To help promote and highlight their events, the site includes a jukebox filled with Bamboo Room regulars, fully-manageable calendar, full drink menu and photo tour. Union also finished a portfolio-based site and logo redesign for Cox Group, a PR firm located in Mooresville, NC. The site showcases the company's wide-ranging abilities and extensive body of work.

Jan 2005

Focus on Redesigning Communication

In Company News by Banks Wilson

In February of 2002, inside a small office building in South Charlotte, Banks Wilson started on a quest to build his own design firm aimed at “Redesigning Communication.” After realizing agency work wasn’t for him, Wilson knew starting his own firm was a logical choice and Union (formerly Studiobanks) was born.

Wilson attended the University of North Carolina at Charlotte concentrating his studies in graphic design. Upon graduation in the spring of 2001, Wilson felt his talents were underutilized working in a corporate environment.
