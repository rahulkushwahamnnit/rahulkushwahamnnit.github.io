    Custom-made for Dog Lovers

    When you have a pet product line as innovative as Dublin Dog’s, you want a website that follows suit. That was exactly what the Union team was tasked to accomplish, when Dublin Dog approached us with the opportunity to create a fresh e-commerce experience for the brand.

    The goal was to create an updated Magento-driven platform, which would leverage the latest e-commerce technologies to showcase Dublin Dog’s durable, eco-friendly dog-lover products, while integrating and supporting key organizational systems.

  • 02 06

    Brand Development

    The Union team started by taking an in-depth look at Dublin Dog’s brand, product line, their business objectives and goals, their marketing messages and past efforts, and their social fan base.

    With this unique insight into the brand and an understanding of where their team wished to go, Union set out to implement a strategic plan that would elevate brand messages, leverage a more impactful online product-marketing experience, employ fan-generated social assets and sharing — all while streamlining the path to purchase.

  • 03 06

    Designing the Online Store

    Our challenge was not only to create an engaging e-commerce experience for the brand, but converge all of Dublin Dog’s strategic needs into a system that would respond to its mobile buyers and deliver that same experience across all connected devices.

    A Brand-New Look

    Through a modern approach to design, we worked to shape and infuse the buying experience with a clean look enhanced by crisp product graphics and imagery — meshing the voice of the brand, dynamic lifestyle photography, product imagery, fan-generated pictures and an intuitive user interface. Then, that same great look was leveraged across Dublin Dog's social media platforms.

    All Style — No Stink

    Union’s team took to our love for dogs and created an experience that truly delivered on Dublin Dog's tagline, “Products that look as good as they perform.”

    Campaign

    As a companion effort to our digital platform work, Union developed and deployed a multilayered digital marketing campaign that leveraged flexible, attention-grabbing display, remarketing and paid search ads, as well as Dublin Dog's own email audience. Over the course of our campaign efforts, Dublin Dog experienced a massive improvement in digital campaign performance metrics.
