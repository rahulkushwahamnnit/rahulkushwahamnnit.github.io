About

An exciting event for the smarty pants who know their courses deep enough. From visuals to Audio, this time the quizzing event will bring about an energy never seen before.

Participation Rules

• A team can have minimum of two and maximum of three students. It is advisable that teams have members from all three years, but it is not mandatory.

• The participants will have to go through elimination round before making it to the final day event.

Event Format

•Preliminary Rounds
   - The written round will be an elimination round.
   - The paper will consist of 30 questions in a time limit of 45 minutes that may have MCQs, puzzles and cross words.

Final Rounds
   - The first round will be an elimination round.
   - Participants need to be ready for pop quiz, audio/visual round.
   - A rapid fire round will lead to the event closure.

Quiz format:
The questions will test your applicable knowledge and presence of mind. A mugged up course structure may not be exactly helpful.

Rules and Regulations

• Calculators are not allowed for any calculation oriented questions.

• Use of unfair means will lead to disqualification of the team.

• The preliminary round is essentially elimination round. Its marks will not influence your ranking in further rounds.

• On the final day event, every round will be evaluated for individual score.

• In case of tie, a tie-breaker will follow only if the cumulative scores of all the rounds also lead to a tie.
