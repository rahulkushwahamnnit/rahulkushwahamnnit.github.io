Event Format

First Round (Elimination Round)

There will be a written elimination test which will be based on questions related to current affairs, Business Field and other such issues.


Second Round

Sub- round 1: (Buzzer Round) Questions will be displayed in front of the teams and within certain time limit they will have to give answers to those questions.


Sub-round 2: (Visual Round) In this round Teams will be bombarded with some questions with few options as answers and if they fail to give the right answer in the first attempt they will be offered second chance to answer the same question and still if they fail then there will be negative marking.


Third Round (Stage Round) In this round the teams will be given the option to choose from the various fields such as sports, politics, business etc and they will be asked questions based on that field only.
