Event Description

If you are one of the few who exceptionally stay stern to the belief that you have to somehow return the blessings of being blessed by the environment since ages,
if you are among those few who still haven’t started to overlook the ‘save environment’ articles in magazines and newspapers, then you are exactly the one we are looking for!

Don't simply inhale oxygen, with it also inhale the responsibility of saving its source. Prepare a working model, product or device which can protect nature from hazardous elements and strive to create a harmless environment. Use your engineering skills & present your idea.

Event Format

• Participants have to submit an abstract in pdf/doc format explaining their model, product or device with in 200 words by 22nd September 2014

• Further shortlisted candidates on the basis of their abstracts have to send a presentation which should highlight advantages, feasibility, functionality, SWOT analysis, brief cost analysis and most importantly the Environmental Impact of their idea.

• Final shortlisted candidates are required to implement/make a working model, product or device which they have to present at the time of Avishkar 2k14. There will be questionnaire round at the end of presentation.

Rules and Regulations

• No of rounds-3

• Team-3 students (at max). Two students from chemical engineering branch are compulsory.

• You have to mail your abstract with your team details at rasayans2k14@gmail.com before 20th Sept 2014.

• After abstract submission round students will be provided mentors for helping in the implementation of their idea.

• Eliminations will be there in each round.
