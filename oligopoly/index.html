Introduction

The critical ingredient is getting off your butt and doing something. A lot of people have ideas, but there are few who decide to do something about them now. Not tomorrow. Not next week. But today. The true entrepreneur is a doer, not a dreamer.


What is a startup?

1) Find a problem

2) Find a solution

3) Change that solution into product or service

4) Find investors or bootstrap

5) Make products

6) Sell your products

7) Make money


But here we are missing a most important part of whole process. Writing a business plan is backbone of any business idea. Ideas are easy, implementation is hard and only way to make it easy is writing a business plan.

Event Description

Do you have a BIG idea? Want to make it revolutionary? What do you do next? Implementation is the only solution to make it revolutionary. Entrepreneurship is to shape an idea into realty. Writing a business plan is an initial step of startup. Takeoff is business plan competition for college students. If you have a dream but are starved for start-up capital, a business-plan competition could put you and your company on the map.

If you have a revolutionary business Idea or want to change the society then Take-off is a platform to validate your idea and starting up immediately. To participate in Take-off, you have to submit a business plan for your idea.

Event Format

• Participants have to submit an abstract in pdf/doc format explaining their Idea, product/service with in 200 words before 8th Oct 2014, 11:59 pm.

• Further shortlisted candidates on the basis of their abstracts have to submit a business plan and presentation on front of the Take-off Judges.

• Personal Interviews.

Eligibility
